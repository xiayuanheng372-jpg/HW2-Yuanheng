# Week 2: Build and Evaluate a Simple GenAI Workflow

## Business Workflow
Drafting customer support responses for an e-commerce company. When a customer submits a support request, the system generates a professional, empathetic draft reply for the support agent to review and send.

## User
Customer support agents at an e-commerce company who handle a high volume of incoming customer messages daily.

## Input
A raw customer message (complaint, question, or request), along with optional context such as order status or product category.

## Output
A polished, professional draft reply that acknowledges the customer's concern, provides a helpful response, and maintains a friendly tone.

## Why Automate This?
Support agents spend significant time drafting routine replies to common issues (shipping delays, refund requests, product questions). An LLM-assisted draft reduces response time, ensures consistent tone, and lets agents focus on complex or escalated cases instead of repetitive writing.
